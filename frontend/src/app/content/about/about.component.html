About Us

THERE IS MERIT IN PURSUING PERFECTION

THE ART OF COFFEE

Roasting coffee to perfection is exacting, but not mysterious. Here's how we do it:

1) Travel the globe to find the best coffee beans.

2) Buy traceable lots of great beans at a fair price.

3) Roast with artistry and advanced technology. Buy traceable lots of great bean at a fair price.

4) Control quality from grower to roaster to barista to consumer.

SMALLER IS BETTER

Many of our speciality beans come from a single farm or even from a specific area on the farm, so they are limited in quantity and superior in quality.

We buy directly from the farmers and have established strong, long-term relationships, based on good coffee and fair prices.
